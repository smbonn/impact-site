About ImpACT Consulting

Intentional strategy. Authentic growth. Real impact.

At ImpACT Consulting, we believe every bold idea deserves the structure to thrive. Whether you're stepping into leadership, launching something new, or refining your voice, we help you move with purpose, not just momentum. Through personalized coaching, strategic consulting, and dynamic trainings, we empower individuals and organizations to align their work with their values and amplify their impact.

Mission

To empower individuals, organizations, and communities to create measurable, meaningful impact through intentional strategy, capacity-building, and authentic leadership development.

Vision

A world where every idea with potential becomes a catalyst for change, where values-driven leaders and organizations are equipped to lead with clarity, purpose, and sustainable impact.

Who we work with

Our clients range from first-time founders and college students to nonprofit leaders, small business owners, titleholders, and public sector professionals. What they all have in common: they're ready to grow, and they want to do it with intention.

Our approach

We don't do cookie-cutter. We listen, adapt, and co-create strategies that reflect your goals and values. Every session, every training, and every coaching conversation is rooted in clarity over chaos, confidence without ego, and actionable plans, not vague advice. We believe impact isn't accidental, it's designed.

Meet the founder

Sierra Marie Bonn

Founder. Strategist. Speaker. Advocate for Impact.

Sierra Marie Bonn is a community-first consultant, nonprofit founder, and award-winning public speaker based in El Dorado, Kansas, with a passion for helping people turn ideas into action. With a background in engineering technology, innovation design, and digital strategy, she blends systems thinking with storytelling to help leaders grow with clarity, purpose, and measurable results.

As the founder of Let's Go Full STEAM Ahead!, a national nonprofit focused on STEAM education and gender equity, Sierra has spent years empowering the next generation of innovators. Through ImpACT Consulting, she works with emerging entrepreneurs, mission-driven professionals, and changemakers who want to lead boldly and grow sustainably, and with individuals and organizations preparing for high-stakes interviews and competitive processes, including scholarship pageant systems such as Miss Walnut Valley and Miss Dodge City. Her experience spans small business development, education, pageantry, and workforce development, giving her a unique, intersectional lens to support her clients' diverse goals.

At her core, Sierra is driven by a belief in the power of people with potential. She's committed to creating spaces where others feel equipped, empowered, and energized to grow, not just professionally, but personally and with purpose. Her clients often describe her as a "strategic sounding board," someone who listens deeply, asks the right questions, and delivers real, actionable tools.

Why she started ImpACT Consulting

Sierra saw a gap between passion and execution. So many brilliant people were burning out trying to make a difference, without the right tools, clarity, or support. She created ImpACT Consulting to close that gap and help people lead, serve, and grow on purpose.

When she's not consulting or coaching, you'll find her reading in a cozy chair, building educational resources, planning events, or hosting delicious dinner parties.

Values she leads with: Integrity | Strategy | Equity | Empowerment | Intentionality
