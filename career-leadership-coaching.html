Career Strategy & Leadership Coaching

You don't need generic advice. You need someone who will actually look at what you're doing, understand where you want to go, and help you close the gap. ImpACT Consulting's career and leadership coaching is built for people who are serious about their next move, whether that's a competitive job, a leadership promotion, a scholarship, or a brand that finally reflects the work you're doing.

We work with early professionals, career pivoters, executives stepping into new roles, and leaders who want their professional presence to match their actual impact.

Our coaching philosophy

Effective coaching should do more than prepare you for one moment. It should help you develop skills that strengthen how you think, communicate, and lead over time. At ImpACT Consulting, our approach emphasizes clarity, reflection, and strategic growth. Rather than relying on generic advice, we work together to identify the strengths you already bring to the table and refine how you apply them in meaningful ways. The result is preparation that feels authentic, thoughtful, and sustainable long after the initial opportunity has passed.

Our coaching areas

Career Strategy & Interview Coaching

Strong opportunities often begin with strong preparation. This coaching focuses on helping you present your experience clearly, communicate your strengths effectively, and approach interviews with confidence. The goal: you walk into that interview or application knowing exactly how to present yourself, and why it works.

  • Resume review and strategic positioning
  • Interview preparation with structured practice
  • Application strategy for competitive programs
  • LinkedIn alignment and professional branding
  • Personalized job search or internship strategy

Ideal for students, early professionals, career pivoters, and anyone preparing for an important opportunity.

Personal Brand Development

Your professional reputation is built through the way you communicate your ideas, share your expertise, and position your work. This coaching focuses on helping you clarify your message, define your leadership voice, and build visibility in ways that feel authentic and intentional. The goal: your name and your work become synonymous in your space.

  • Brand clarity and message development
  • Visual and narrative alignment across platforms
  • Thought leadership and content strategy
  • Audience engagement and visibility planning

Ideal for professionals, entrepreneurs, and emerging leaders who want their work to be recognized and remembered.

Strategic Communication & Public Speaking

Whether you are speaking on stage, leading meetings, or participating in interviews, the ability to communicate clearly and confidently is one of the most valuable leadership skills you can develop. The goal: you stop bracing for high-stakes conversations and start owning them.

  • Executive presence development
  • Speech and presentation coaching
  • Keynote and pitch preparation
  • Media interview preparation
  • Panel and presentation delivery practice

Ideal for leaders, speakers, founders, and professionals who want to communicate with clarity and authority.

Executive Coaching

Leadership brings decisions that don't come with a manual. Executive coaching creates the space to work through them, with clarity, not chaos. We work with executives, nonprofit directors, and civic leaders navigating organizational complexity, managing transitions, or preparing to lead at a higher level. Every engagement is structured around your specific challenges, not a generic program.

  • Strategic decision-making frameworks for high-stakes situations
  • Executive presence and leadership communication coaching
  • Team alignment and conflict navigation
  • Transition planning for new roles or expanded responsibilities
  • Preparing for boards, media, and high-visibility opportunities

Ideal for executives, nonprofit leaders, and professionals stepping into significant leadership roles.

Who we work with

If you are preparing for an important opportunity, navigating a transition, or looking to strengthen your leadership presence, coaching can provide the structure and perspective needed to move forward with confidence. Coaching sessions are available virtually for clients nationwide.
